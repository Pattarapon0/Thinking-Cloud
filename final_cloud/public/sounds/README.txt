Add the following sound effect files in this directory:

1. click.mp3 - Short click sound for when text is clicked
2. vanish.mp3 - Pop/disappear sound for when text is removed
3. collision.mp3 - Bounce/bump sound for when text collides
4. join.mp3 - Pleasant notification sound for when a peer joins
5. leave.mp3 - Subtle notification sound for when a peer leaves

Sound requirements:
- All sounds should be short (less than 1 second)
- Format: MP3
- Recommended volume: normalized around -6dB
- Clear, non-intrusive sounds that won't disrupt gameplay
